About Indian Premier League

The Indian Premier League (IPL) is a professional Twenty20 cricket league founded in 2008 by the Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI). It has become one of the most popular sports leagues globally.

Tournament Format

The IPL follows a round-robin and playoff format. Teams compete in league matches, and the top teams qualify for playoffs leading to the final.

Rules & Regulations

  • Each team plays league matches against other teams
  • Points awarded for wins and no-results
  • Top teams qualify for playoffs
  • Final match decides the IPL champion

Purpose of IPLStats

IPLStats is built as an educational frontend project to understand DOM manipulation, UI design, and basic JavaScript data handling.
